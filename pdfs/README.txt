DROP BRAND GUIDELINE PDFs HERE
================================

Naming convention (match the project ID in projects.js):
  verde-kitchen-guidelines.pdf
  crest-legal-guidelines.pdf
  brickell-coffee-guidelines.pdf
  dade-dental-guidelines.pdf
  (etc.)

After adding a PDF, update projects.js:
  pdfUrl: "pdfs/verde-kitchen-guidelines.pdf",

The drawer will automatically show a "View Brand Guidelines" button.
